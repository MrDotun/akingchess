Our Story

The Legend of AKINGCHESS

Founder: Akan Udoh

AKINGCHESS was founded by Akan Udoh with a singular vision: to bring world-class chess instruction to every school in Nigeria. As a premier academy, we believe chess is more than a game—it is a tool for developing strategic minds and disciplined leaders.

What We Do

We provide comprehensive chess instruction services, working with a large network of trained instructors in schools across the nation. Our activities include:

  • Professional School Coaching
  • Youth Chess Tournaments
  • Private Mentorship
  • Quality Chess Equipment Supply
